Privacy Policy

BU Today Agency | Effective date: 20 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BU Today Agency ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our website, submit forms, request audits, or use our automation and support services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, business name, and location.
  • Business and enquiry information you submit in forms, onboarding flows, and messages.
  • Operational data required to provide services (for example workflow metadata and support logs).
  • Technical information such as browser/device signals, IP address, and usage events.

2. Why We Process Information

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver requested services.
  • To create and deliver audits, onboarding, automation systems, and support.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

3. Legal Basis

We process information based on consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and legal obligations, as applicable under POPIA and other relevant laws.

4. Sharing and Third Parties

We may use trusted service providers for infrastructure, communications, analytics, payments, and hosting. These providers process information only as needed to deliver services and under confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Cross-Border Processing

Some providers may process data outside South Africa. Where this occurs, we apply contractual, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate to the risk profile.

6. Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably required for service delivery, security, legal compliance, and dispute resolution. Data that is no longer required is deleted or irreversibly anonymized.

7. Security

We apply layered safeguards including access control, environment separation, encrypted transport, audit logging, and least-privilege operational access. No system is risk-free, but we continuously improve controls based on threat and compliance requirements.

8. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or objection to certain processing. You may also request details of how your information is processed.
