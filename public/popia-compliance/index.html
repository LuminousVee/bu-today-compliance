POPIA Compliance

BU Today Agency | Effective date: 20 February 2026
Protection of Personal Information Act (South Africa)

BU Today Agency applies a privacy-by-design approach aligned with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This page summarizes how we apply POPIA principles when delivering services and operating our systems.

1. Accountability

We assign responsibility for personal information governance and maintain internal controls for lawful processing, secure handling, and incident response.

2. Processing Limitation

  • We process personal information for specific, explicit, and legitimate business purposes.
  • We minimize collection to data that is necessary for service delivery and support.
  • Where consent is required, it is requested and documented appropriately.

3. Purpose Specification and Retention

Information is retained only as long as required for contracted services, legal obligations, and operational security. Obsolete data is deleted or de-identified.

4. Further Processing Limitation

Any further processing is assessed for compatibility with the original purpose and legal basis.

5. Information Quality

We take reasonable steps to maintain information accuracy and relevance based on the purpose of processing.

6. Openness and Transparency

We provide clear notices on processing activities and how to contact us regarding data rights.

7. Security Safeguards

  • Role-based access control and least-privilege access.
  • Secure transmission channels and operational logging.
  • Service-provider controls and confidentiality requirements.
  • Incident management process for containment, assessment, and notification where required.

8. Data Subject Participation

Subject to POPIA and other applicable law, data subjects may request access, correction, deletion, objection, or restriction of processing.

9. Operator and Third-Party Management

Third-party operators are selected based on technical and organizational controls and are required to process data under contractual safeguards.

10. Contact and Complaints

For POPIA-related requests, contact: believersutopia@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the South African Information Regulator.
