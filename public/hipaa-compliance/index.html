HIPAA Compliance

BU Today Agency | Effective date: 20 February 2026

For healthcare-related projects, BU Today Agency implements controls aligned to HIPAA security and privacy expectations where applicable to the service scope and contractual obligations.

1. Scope

HIPAA obligations depend on the role and data context. Where services involve Protected Health Information (PHI), requirements are addressed through contracted controls and role-appropriate security measures.

2. Administrative Safeguards

  • Access governance and least-privilege controls.
  • Documented procedures for onboarding, offboarding, and role changes.
  • Operational monitoring and incident response process.

3. Technical Safeguards

  • Secure transport for data in transit.
  • Authentication controls and environment separation.
  • Audit logging and operational traceability for support activities.

4. Physical and Infrastructure Controls

Infrastructure is hosted with managed providers that maintain physical and environmental security controls suitable for cloud operations.

5. Incident Response and Notification

We maintain an incident process for detection, containment, impact analysis, remediation, and notification aligned with contractual and legal obligations.

6. Vendor and Subprocessor Governance

Service providers are selected based on security posture and contractual obligations. Additional controls may be applied based on project sensitivity.

7. Client Responsibilities

Clients remain responsible for configuration choices, user account governance, internal policies, and legal determinations specific to their regulatory status.

8. Contact

Compliance and security queries: believersutopia@gmail.com
